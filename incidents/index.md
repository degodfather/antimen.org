# Notable Incidents

This section documents notable incidents from across India where men and children became victims of violence, abuse, neglect, false accusations, custodial disputes, or systemic failures.

They serve as reminders that suffering, victimization, and injustice are not limited to one gender.

Many of these incidents received significant public attention, exposed gaps in existing laws and policies, or highlighted concerns raised by fathers' rights and men's rights advocates.

By preserving and documenting these cases, we hope to encourage evidence-based discussions about child welfare, equal protection under the law, due process, and the need to recognize all victims regardless of gender.
